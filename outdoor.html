Outdoor Games

Explore some fun outdoor activities to enjoy with friends and family!

1. Treasure Hunt

Organize a treasure hunt by hiding small objects around the yard or park. Use clues and riddles to guide players to the treasures.

2. Tag

A classic game where one player is 'it' and tries to tag others while they run around. The tagged player becomes the next 'it'.

3. Sack Race

Grab some burlap sacks or large pillowcases and have a hopping race. This is perfect for groups and family events!

4. Frisbee

Play a casual game of catch with a frisbee, or set up goals for a fun and competitive frisbee match.

5. Obstacle Course

Create a backyard obstacle course using items like chairs, ropes, cones, and hula hoops. Time each participant to see who finishes fastest!
